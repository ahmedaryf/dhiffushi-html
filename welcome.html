Welcome to Dhiffushi

Diffushi Maldives - The Sunrise Island, holds the distinction of being the first
local island to witness the sunrise in the Maldives,
positioned as the most northern eastern local island in North Male' Atoll.
Renowned as a popular beach destination,
the island boasts a rapidly growing tourism sector and
a vast lagoon area ideal for various watersports.
